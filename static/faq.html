Day To Day Usage

"Is it ready to be used as a daily driver OS?"

Resoundingly yes, Graphene OS is stable and feature-rich enough to be utilized as a daily driver. An app store like Yalp or F-Droid is not included, but can be added by downloading and installing the required .apk.
